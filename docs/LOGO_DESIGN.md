# ChessXL Logo Design Specification

## Overview
The logo for **ChessXL** should bridge the gap between a high-end personal training tool and the aesthetic of the **ChessUp Pro** projection app. It needs to feel premium, modern, and "intelligent."

## Core Concepts
1. **The Santa Figure**:
   - The central character is Santa Claus.
   - **Eyes**: A haunting or high-tech aesthetic where Santa has no pupils—just solid white/glowing eyes.
   - This represents a "God-mode" or "All-seeing" AI coach.

2. **Chess Integration**:
   - Santa might be holding a chess piece or wearing a crown/hat that incorporates chess motifs.
   - Minimalist, high-contrast style.

3. **Projection/Grid Motif** (Reference to ChessUp Pro):
   - Subtle geometric patterns or digital light beams around the character to hint at the "projection/AR" heritage.
   - A mix of traditional iconography with futuristic digital elements.

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Dark, edgy, premium. 
- **Shape**: A rounded square (squircle) for the app icon container.

## Color Palette (Aligned with App Theme)
- **Primary Teal**: `#0D9488` (Main brand color)
- **Accent Gold/Amber**: `#F59E0B` (Used for the glowing eyes or highlights)
- **Deep Slate**: `#0F172A` (Background/Shadows for depth)
- **True White**: For the glowing eyes.
